## Corporate identity (absolute)
- Parent identity of the platform: **Rukn Al-Tawfir Cosmetic Trading (ركن التوفير كوزمتك للتجارة)**.
- Optional parent group reference: **Aljaradi Medpower Group**.
- iSiS, SEKEM, Steviola, NO CAL, Monivo, Baby Tawfir, Bambo Fresh = represented brands / strategic partners only. They live inside the Brand Ecosystem and NEVER replace the corporate identity.
- The site never reads as "iSiS Group's website".

## Catalog & pricing
- Catalogs = content source. Public pages may show: descriptions, ingredients, benefits, variants, pack sizes, usage, technical info.
- NEVER display: prices, wholesale prices, internal commercial data.
- Catalog PDF visibility is CMS-managed: public / restricted / B2B-only.

## Asset governance
- Uploaded logos are canonical; never regenerate or restyle them.
- Uploaded packaging photography is preserved as-is.
- Baby Tawfir packaging may contain infant imagery (approved as official artwork).
- Zero-Faces rule applies ONLY to newly generated banners, illustrations, promotional graphics — NOT to official packaging.

## Language & layout
- Arabic-first, RTL default. English as secondary.

## Conversion model
- No e-commerce checkout. Primary CTA = WhatsApp inquiry (774040383). Secondary = B2B partnership form.

## Execution
- Phased delivery; explicit user approval required between phases.
